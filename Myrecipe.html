Sweet beer Recipe

Malawi is home to various foods but amongst them all, my favorite is Sweet beer.
It is brewed from maize and millet flour giving the drinker a smooth, salivating, once ina life time experience.
this brief recipe outlines how to brew this sweet traditional drink so as share the experience to everyone.

Ingredients

  • Maize flour
  • Millet Flour
  • Water
  • Sugar (Variable)

Instructions

  1. Warm 1 liter of Water
  2. Add 1/2 kg of flour to the water and stir slowly until the mixture is relatively thick
  3. If the porridge (mixture above) is not thick enough add more flour or water if too thick
  4. Let the mixture boil for 15 minutes
  5. Mix the millet flour in water
  6. Add the water-millet-flour mixture to the porridge and let it simmer until it is thick, but flowing
  7. let the mixture cool
  8. Enjoy!!
